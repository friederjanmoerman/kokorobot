I am Rekka Bellum, an illustrator from Montreal, living on a small sailboat. I do art for games, childrens books and boardgames

My first game, Oquonie was nominated for an IGF award in 2015 for visual excellence.

I am co-creator of the traveling studio Hundred Rabbits, and manage a creative cooking blog called GrimGrains.

My past work experience include working in studio for Sava Transmedia, Pascal Blais animation, Hiberbum Creations, Tele Quebec and TonePlus animation studios. I have done contract work for Edoki Academy, Volta Creations and Scorpion Masque.

What is Wiktopher

Wiktopher is a fiction story taking placing in a fantasy world, consisting of roughly 60,000 words. The novel will be in black and white, and have an illustrated glossary, a language alphabet (with songs) a world map and recipes. All of these, will permit the reader to learn of the world and intricacies of each region visited by the main characters. A noteworthy aspect of this story, it that it's written without using gender pronouns.

The glossary will contain all of the (fictional) flora and fauna mentioned in the text. Recipes will appear when there is mention of cooking (with cooking instructions).

In the text, there will be songs written in the whistled language of ilken. The reader will be able to learn this language, and whistle the songs, with the instructions provided in the text.

It's time to unlearn.

The goal of gender-neutral writing, is to eliminate our tendency to modify our expectations of characters - and irl people - based on their genders. My life has been strewn with such behaviour modifications from others. Wiktopher, is for me, a means of teaching people to break this habit.

The world has a lot of unlearning to do.
